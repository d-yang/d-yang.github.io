Hello! I am Diane (Ya Han) Yang, a Taiwanese-born designer that also considers California to be my hometown. I currently reside in Baltimore, studying at Maryland Institute College of Art to pursue for a BFA in Graphic Design.

I am a full time student and a part-time freelance designer, including logo, branding, various graphics, web design, and user interface design. All websites I design and code are responsive and mobile friendly with a clean user interface. I am very familiar with the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Dreamweaver, as well as basic HTML/CSS.
